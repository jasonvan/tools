Technical Specifications

How the conversion process works

Processing Method

This tool resizes PDF pages while preserving aspect ratio. Content is scaled to fit the new page size and centered automatically using client-side PDF manipulation.

Known Limitations

  • Form fillable PDFs: form fields may be adjusted or misaligned when scaled
  • Encrypted or password-protected PDFs are not supported
  • Maximum file size is 50MB per document
  • Mixed page sizes will be converted uniformly to target format
  • Output files may be larger than originals (no compression applied)

Privacy Notice: All processing happens locally in your browser. No files are uploaded to any server. Your documents remain completely private and never leave your device.
